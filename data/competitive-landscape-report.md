## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The San Juan / Puerto Rico tours SERP is **moderately competitive** with a clear dominant player (sanjuanpuertorico.com) and a rising challenger (wanderpuertorico.com). However, the landscape is **heavily fragmented** — no single site owns the comparison/decision-support space. Marketplaces (Viator, Tripadvisor, GetYourGuide) dominate transactional queries but leave editorial comparison wide open. San Juan Excursions' **first-person guide persona (Mateo Rivera)** is a unique differentiator no competitor currently owns.

**Key Findings:**
- **30+ identified competitors** across 6 categories (affiliate blogs, marketplaces, local operators, travel blogs, DMO, aggregators)
- **sanjuanpuertorico.com** is the #1 authority but has weak comparison tables and no personal guide persona
- **wanderpuertorico.com** is the fastest-rising competitor with excellent content strategy (experience-type framing, pricing transparency)
- **islandjourneys.com** is a hybrid (operator + blog) doing comparison content well
- **Multiple new WordPress-based affiliate blogs** entering the space (escapetrails8, travelzenith2, vacationrealm5)
- **8 high-value content gaps** identified with low-to-medium competition
- **San Juan Excursions' 29-page site** already covers core topics but needs expansion into identified gaps

### 3.3 Pattern Insights

1. **sanjuanpuertorico.com dominates with flat, descriptive URLs** — but this creates a cluttered sitemap. Their URLs are long but keyword-rich: `/el-yunque-rainforest-tours-from-san-juan/`
2. **wanderpuertorico.com's blog structure** (`/blog/{slug}.html`) is clean but less hierarchical — all content treated equally
3. **puertorico.com's location hierarchy** (`/san-juan/tours/`) is logical for island-wide coverage but less focused
4. **Our pattern** (`/{category}/{slug}/`) is the most structured for SEO siloing — each category hub builds topical authority for sub-pages
