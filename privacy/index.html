Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

San Juan Excursions uses minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how people use the site. Here's exactly what that means.

What We Collect

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with IP anonymisation enabled to understand basic, aggregate things: how many people visit, which pages are useful, roughly which country and device they came from. This helps us decide what to write next. We cannot identify individual visitors from this data.

We also use GoatCounter, a lightweight, privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use persistent cookies or store personal data. It counts page views and referrers only.

The only cookies this site sets are the ones Google Analytics uses to tell a returning visitor from a new one. There are no advertising cookies, no tracking pixels from ad networks, and no social media widgets. If you block cookies in your browser, the site works fine.

What We Don't Collect

No personal data collection. No user accounts. No newsletter signups. No payment information (all bookings happen on Viator's platform). No advertising networks. No fingerprinting scripts. No social media pixels. The site is static HTML served by Vercel — there is no database and no server-side data collection beyond standard server logs.

Affiliate Links

Our links to Viator use standard tracking parameters (PID and MCID) to attribute any bookings you make. Viator may place a cookie for commission attribution purposes. We have no access to that cookie or any data Viator collects. See Viator's privacy policy.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for operational and security purposes. See Vercel's privacy policy.

Contact

Questions? Email mateo@san-juan-excursions.com.

Data Retention

Anonymised analytics data is retained by Google for up to 26 months (our GA4 data retention setting). GoatCounter retains aggregated page view counts indefinitely but deletes individual visitor session data after 8 hours. If you've emailed us, we keep your correspondence for reference unless you request deletion.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR (EU/EEA), CCPA (California), or similar regulations. These include the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we collect almost no personal data, exercising these rights is straightforward. Under GDPR, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Cookie Management

The GA4 cookies (_ga, _ga_*) are first-party cookies with a maximum lifespan of 2 years. You can block them in your browser settings without affecting site functionality. Most browsers also offer "Do Not Track" settings. We honour DNT signals where technically feasible. For detailed cookie controls, consult your browser's privacy documentation or use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on Vercel, which maintains standard server access logs for security and operational monitoring. These logs may include IP addresses, timestamps, and user-agent strings. Vercel's data processing is governed by their Data Processing Agreement. All tour bookings occur on Viator's platform — we never handle payment data. See Viator's privacy policy for their data practices.

Changes to This Policy

We update this page when our data practices change. The last-updated date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be noted here. This is a static site with no user accounts, so we cannot email you about policy updates.
