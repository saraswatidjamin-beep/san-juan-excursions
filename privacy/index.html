Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

San Juan Excursions takes your privacy seriously. This site does not collect, store, or process personal information unless you voluntarily provide it by emailing us directly.

What We Don't Collect

We use no cookies for tracking. No third-party analytics. No advertising networks. No fingerprinting scripts. No social media pixels. The site is static HTML served by Vercel — there is no database, no user accounts, and no server-side data collection.

Affiliate Links

Our links to Viator use standard tracking parameters (PID and MCID) to attribute any bookings you make. Viator may place a cookie for commission attribution purposes. We have no access to that cookie or any data Viator collects. See Viator's privacy policy.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for operational and security purposes. See Vercel's privacy policy.
